Hobbies & Interests

Computer Vision Experiments

I do computer vision experiments on the side, building small projects around image processing and CNNs, trying my hand at tasks like object detection and segmentation. It's challenging, but there's something deeply satisfying about teaching a machine to see.

Machine Learning Deep Dives

I tend to pick one concept and go deep, whether it's tracing through the calculus behind backpropagation, building intuition for better visualizing loss landscapes, or getting hands-on with tabular data preprocessing. Less breadth, more depth. That's how it sticks.

Exercise and Fitness

Mostly weightlifting and the occasional run. I've recently just got back to it but I've always felt exercising keeps me grounded. It's the one part of the day that's entirely offline.

Following Motorsports

F1, WEC, and NASCAR. I'm drawn to the engineering as much as the racing. The strategy, the margins, and the obsessive pursuit of speed is inspiring. Motorsports is an expensive passion to take, but one day.

Single-Player Gaming

I prefer single-player games on console or PC with strong narratives, deep mechanics, and good graphics, the kind that rewards exploration. It's one of the few hobbies where getting lost in the world and story is entirely the point.
